About us

Welcome to Dining Delight

Situated in the heart of the capital, is a colossal beauty for all food lovers. It is the epitome of class and elegance, with interiors adorned by paintings of monuments, the space is just beautiful. Serving some of the best Italian and Continental dishes from its affordable menu, Local makes it a worthwhile visit for one and all. The exclusive menu also offers an array of exclusive dishes to choose from.

Spending some good time with your friends or unwinding with your colleagues, here is where you can head to for some unadulterated happiness!
